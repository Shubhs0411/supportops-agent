# Safety and Abuse Policy

## Overview
We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users.

## Prohibited Behavior
- Harassment, bullying, or threats
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Spam or fraudulent activity
- Impersonation or identity theft
- Distribution of harmful content

## Reporting
Users can report abuse through:
1. In-app reporting tools
2. Support ticket system
3. Direct email to trust and safety team

## Response
- Reports are reviewed within 24 hours
- Urgent safety concerns are prioritized
- Violations may result in account suspension or termination
- Law enforcement may be contacted for serious violations

## Appeals
Users may appeal moderation decisions through our support system.

## Contact
For safety concerns, contact our Trust & Safety team immediately.
