# Refund Policy

## Overview
Our refund policy ensures fair and transparent processing of refund requests.

## Eligibility
- Refunds are available for purchases made within 30 days
- Digital products may have different refund terms
- Subscription cancellations are prorated

## Process
1. Submit a refund request through our support portal
2. Our team reviews the request within 2-3 business days
3. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method

## Exceptions
- Refunds cannot be issued for services already consumed
- Custom orders may not be eligible for refunds
- Refunds are subject to our terms of service

## Contact
For refund inquiries, contact our billing support team.
