# Account Recovery Policy

## Overview
We provide secure account recovery options for users who have lost access to their accounts.

## Recovery Methods
1. Email-based password reset
2. Security question verification
3. Two-factor authentication recovery
4. Manual account verification for complex cases

## Process
- Password reset requests are processed immediately via email
- Security question verification requires correct answers
- Two-factor recovery may take 24-48 hours for verification
- Manual recovery requires identity verification documents

## Security
- All recovery requests are logged for security purposes
- Suspicious activity may trigger additional verification
- Accounts with security concerns may be temporarily locked

## Best Practices
- Keep your recovery email up to date
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication when available

## Contact
For account recovery issues, contact our identity support team.
